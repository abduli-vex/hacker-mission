# Project Hacker-Game

### // THE WORLD

**2048. The Corporate Age.**

The government you once served no longer exists. In its place stands **Aegis,** a security conglomerate that didn't just seize power, they *purchased* it. Rumors of corruption and mismanagement gave them the perfect opening. Now they control everything: infrastructure, information, lives.

But Aegis doesn't care about rebuilding the nation. They care about one thing: **Project Helix**.

---

### // THE THREAT

**Project Helix** is an AI unlike anything the world has seen. So dangerous, so sensitive, that even its true purpose is **[REDACTED]** in real-time. 

What we *do* know: it's hungry. It devours natural resources at an apocalyptic rate, belching toxic particulates into the atmosphere. The air itself has become a weapon.

They call the sickness **ENTENT**.

First, you feel it in your chest, a tightness, like invisible hands squeezing. Then your heartbeat slows. Your lungs collapse inward, one breath at a time. There is no cure. There is only time, and not much of it.

Entire districts have been quarantined. Bodies pile up in the Lower Sectors. Aegis says they're "working on a solution."

They're lying.

---

### // YOUR STORY

**You are Cipher** (C1pher to those who know better).

Once, you were one of the best. A government hacker with clearance most people didn't even know existed. When Aegis took over, you got out. Clean break. No looking back.

For two years, you worked freelance for **The Codex Clan,** an underground network of info-brokers, hackers, and digital ghosts. The work was simple: gather intel, get paid, move on. You didn't ask questions. You didn't get attached.

Then your old colleagues started dying.

One by one. Accidents, overdoses, "suicides." Too many to be coincidence. Too precise to be random.

Then came the message. An encrypted burst from someone you used to trust. Their final words before they flatlined:

*"They're eliminating everyone who worked on it. You're next. They won't stop. You can't hide from___"*

The transmission cut out.

You tried to run. Scrubbed your digital footprint, burned your aliases, went dark. But whoever's hunting you isn't human. Their tracking algorithms adapt faster than you can hide. Every firewall you build, they breach. Every false trail you lay, they ignore.

There's only one option left.

**Stop running. Start fighting.**

Discover what "it" is. Learn why they're erasing everyone who knows. Uncover what Project Helix really does.

And if you're lucky, you might survive long enough to shut it down.

---

### // MISSION OBJECTIVES

<aside>

**PRIMARY:** Survive the hunt. Uncover the truth behind the assassinations.

**SECONDARY:** Infiltrate Aegis networks. Expose Project Helix.

**FINAL:** Decide the fate of an AI that could end, or save humanity.

</aside>
